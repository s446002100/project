Course Description

This course provides an in-depth introduction to 3D modelling and animation, equipping students with the technical and creative skills necessary to design and animate 3D assets. Students explore modelling, texturing, rigging, animation, and rendering using Autodesk Maya, Blender, and Unreal Engine while aligning their work with HCI principles.

Main Objectives

  • Master 3D modeling, texturing, rigging, animation, and rendering.
  • Develop hands-on expertise in Maya, Blender, and Unreal Engine.
  • Design assets for games and simulations.
  • Align 3D assets with HCI principles for usability and engagement.

Course Learning Outcomes

Knowledge

  • Understand the fundamental principles of 3D modelling and animation including topology, rigging, and keyframe animation.

Skills

  • Utilize Autodesk Maya and Blender to create 3D assets.
  • Develop realistic and stylized animations using keyframe and procedural techniques.
  • Integrate 3D assets into game engines such as Unreal Engine or Unity.

Course Topics

  • Introduction to 3D Modelling and Environment Setup
  • Modelling Fundamentals (Topology & Edge Flow)
  • Subdivision Modelling and Sculpting
  • Texturing and UV Mapping
  • Basics of Rigging (Bones, Joints, Skinning)
  • Lighting and Rendering
  • Procedural Animation Techniques
  • Integration with Game Engines
  • Principles of Environmental Design
  • Facial Animation and Lip-syncing
  • Optimization Techniques for 3D Assets
  • Keyframes and Interpolation
  • Autodesk Maya Hands-on
  • Blender and Unity for 3D Assets
